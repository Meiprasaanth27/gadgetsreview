Apple Apple Watch Series 8

Rating: ★★★★★

The latest Apple Watch Series 8 has best-in-class health-, fitness-, and wellness-tracking capabilities, powered by accurate heart rate, blood oxygen, and temperature sensors. The timepiece is also the first Apple Watch to have crash detection — an all-new, lifesaving feature. Apple offers the Series 8 with a 41- or 45-millimeter case in a multitude of finishes. Optional cellular network connectivity is available once again. You can customize the timepiece with a wide selection of bands and cases.

Anker 737 Power Bank

Rating: ★★★☆☆

Anker’s 737 Power Bank can charge your laptop via a fast USB-C connector with a maximum power output of 140 watts. It has a battery pack with ample capacity, and rather impressively, a color screen to help you keep tabs on the charging process. The accessory rocks a pair of USB-C ports and a USB-A connector, so you can simultaneously charge up to three devices.

Microsoft Surface Pro 9

Rating: ★★★★☆

Microsoft’s Surface Pro 9 is hands down today’s best 2-in-1 PC. The newcomer has an elegant and versatile design with slim display bezels and top-tier hardware specs. The latter include powerful processors, a vibrant screen with a high refresh rate, and futureproof connectivity options, to name a few. You can order the product in a multitude of configurations in platinum, graphite, forest, and sapphire, with the last two options being new to the Surface Pro lineup. As always, be mindful that the must-have Signature keyboard and Surface Slim Pen 2 cost extra.
